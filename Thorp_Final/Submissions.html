Submissions



After Submitting

After submitting your permission or commission, I will respond within 72 hours to inquire about your request
to let you know if your permission has been granted, or to let you know that I have begun the process of drawing if there are enough details to do so.

Steps to Take If I Have Not Contacted You

If I have not gotten back to you within the given timeframe, you can email me at dthorp1379@gmail.com or message me on Instagram.
I will attempt to return a message as soon as possible. If it is urgent, please let me know in the subject area of the email.

Time

Depending on your commission, it can take anywhere from 3-7 days to finish. Note that there may be other commissions, so please attempt to request a commission at least a week in advance if it is urgent.

Cost?

If granted permission to use my art, it will be free for personal use. Permission to use will be free unless used for business purposes and the cost of commissions is dependent on what is requested.
