Use of Art and Commissions

Before using any of my art for projects, business, personal use, etc., you must first
submit a request for permission. You must leave your name, email, and reason for the request.
If you wish to commission something, you would follow the same process, but you will leave details of your commission instead.
If needed, I will contact you for more details or if I have any questions. You can do the same by contacting me via Instagram or emailing me.
